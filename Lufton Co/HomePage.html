Lufton Co. Village Hall

Located in Somerset, Lufton Co's village hall is a converted 16th centery tithe barn. It has been recently referbished and ready for bookings. Lufton's village hall is a great place to host events, like weddings, partys, social events and many more.

Facilites and services

There are two disabled parking spaces and a further 30 parking spaces on site. There are modern toilets, including disabled facilites and a well equiped kitchen.

The Hall

The hall is 10.5m by 9m with tables to seat 120 or 180 standing. Carpeted floors throughout and exposed roof timbers
